```
Your clipboard just went digital.

Tactics Board gives coaches, players, and analysts a professional canvas to draw plays, set formations, and animate player movements — for 7 sports in one app.

DRAW IT. PLAY IT. SHARE IT.

- Drag players onto accurate, full-color courts and fields
- Draw arrows, lines, and zones with customizable colors and styles
- Tap "Play" to animate player movements step by step
- Save unlimited tactics and share them as crisp images

7 SPORTS. ONE APP.

Soccer (5v5 / 7v7 / 11v11) · Basketball (3v3 / 5v5) · Volleyball (6v6)
Tennis · Badminton · Table Tennis · Pickleball

- Dark theme — easy on the eyes during evening practice
- Works offline — no Wi-Fi on the court? No problem.

11 LANGUAGES

English · 简体中文 · 繁體中文 · 日本語 · 한국어 · Français · Español · Tiếng Việt · ไทย · Bahasa Indonesia · Bahasa Melayu

From youth leagues to national teams — if you coach it, you can draw it.
```

## 9. 截图策略 — 通用框架

### 设计规范

| 参数 | 值 |
|------|-----|
| 尺寸 (iPhone 6.7") | 1290 x 2796 px |
| 尺寸 (iPhone 6.5") | 1242 x 2688 px |
| 尺寸 (iPhone 5.5") | 1242 x 2208 px |
| 尺寸 (iPad 12.9") | 2048 x 2732 px |
| 数量 | 6-8 张 (前 3 张最关键) |
| 背景色 | #0D0D1A (与 App 暗色主题一致) |
| 文字颜色 | #FFFFFF (主标题) / #FFD600 (强调) |
| 字体 | SF Pro Display Bold |
| 截图布局 | 上方 1/3 = 文案标题，下方 2/3 = 设备截图 (带圆角 mockup) |

### 通用 6 张截图顺序

| # | 画面内容 | 目的 |
|---|---------|------|
| 1 | **主阵型展示** — 满场球员 + 标准阵型 | 第一眼冲击力 — "这就是战术板" |
| 2 | **画线功能** — 箭头、跑位线、传球线 | 核心交互展示 |
| 3 | **动画回放** — 播放按钮 + 球员移动轨迹 | 差异化卖点 — 比竞品强 |
| 4 | **阵型选择器** — 弹出面板展示多种阵型 | 内容丰富感 — 开箱即用 |
| 5 | **分享功能** — 导出战术图 | 社交传播暗示 |
| 6 | **多语言/设置** — 11 种语言 | 国际化信任感 |
